About Rota-TechX

Rota-TechX is a 24 hours intercollegiate technical hack and make-athon hosted by Rotaract Club of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering. In this Rota-TechX, the participants are made to choose the problem statements on the Rotary’s 7 Areas of Focus and develop a solution which creates an impact for this society. This event involves pitching in the solution as a product in front of elite panelists.
