About Rota-TechX

RotatechX is a 24-hour intercollegiate hackathon hosted by the Rotaract Club of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering. This event brings together creative minds to solve real-world challenges based on Rotary’s 7 Areas of Focus. Participants brainstorm, develop, and refine their ideas into impactful solutions, which they pitch to a panel of esteemed judges. With innovation and teamwork at its core, RotatechX is a platform for aspiring problem-solvers to showcase their skills and create meaningful change.

Domains

Building peaceful and resilient communities is essential for global stability. This domain focuses on initiatives that prevent conflicts, promote social harmony, and foster inclusive dialogue. Participants can develop solutions that contribute to peace, security, and justice.

Access to quality healthcare is a fundamental need. This domain addresses efforts to prevent diseases, improve treatment methods, and strengthen healthcare infrastructure. Projects can focus on innovative approaches to enhance public health and medical accessibility.

Clean water and proper sanitation are critical for human well-being. This domain emphasizes ensuring access to safe drinking water, improving sanitation facilities, and spreading hygiene awareness. Any solution that contributes to better water management and public health is welcome.

Ensuring the well-being of mothers and children is vital for a healthier society. This domain focuses on reducing maternal and infant mortality, improving healthcare access for mothers, and promoting child nutrition and development. Participants can explore various projects that support this cause.

Education is a powerful tool for empowerment and progress. This domain promotes quality education, literacy, and skill development for all. Any project that enhances learning opportunities, supports teachers, or improves educational accessibility falls under this category.

Strong economies create thriving communities. This domain encourages initiatives that boost entrepreneurship, financial inclusion, and job creation. Solutions that empower local businesses, promote economic self-sufficiency, and foster innovation are part of this focus area.

A sustainable future requires action today. This domain focuses on addressing environmental challenges like climate change, conservation, and sustainable resource management. Any project that contributes to ecological preservation and sustainability is encouraged.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

+

Any undergrade student is eligible to participate in the hackathon.

Is there a registration fee?

+

No, there is no registration fee for this event.

Does this hackathon cover both software and hardware projects?

+

Yes, participants can work on both software and hardware projects.

I am a newbie, can I hack?

+

Absolutely! Beginners are welcome, and this is a great opportunity to learn and collaborate.

What should I bring for the hackathon?

+

Please bring your student ID, laptop, necessary gadgets, and any other materials you may need for your project.

Will there be WiFi facility?

+

Yes, we will provide WiFi access throughout the event.

Can I participate without a team?

+

No, this is a team-based event. Each team must consist of 4 to 6 members.

Will you sponsor our travel costs?

+

Unfortunately, we do not provide reimbursement for travel expenses.
